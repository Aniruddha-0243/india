About India

India is a vast country in South Asia with a population of over 1.3 billion people. It is known for its diverse cultures, languages, and traditions that have evolved over thousands of years.

History

India's history dates back to the Indus Valley Civilization, one of the world's oldest urban cultures. Over millennia, India has been home to many empires including the Maurya, Gupta, Mughal, and British Empires. The country gained independence in 1947 after a long struggle led by figures like Mahatma Gandhi.

Festivals

India celebrates numerous festivals throughout the year, including Diwali, Holi, Eid, Christmas, and many regional festivals that showcase its cultural diversity.

Cuisine

Indian cuisine is known for its bold flavors and diverse dishes. Popular foods include biryani, samosas, dosas, butter chicken, and a variety of vegetarian specialties.
